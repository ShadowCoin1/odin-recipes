Venison Shanks

This dish is rich and decadent. Although the shank is often overlooked if not miligned by most folks. If prepared propery the shank turns into rich delicous hearty meal. Here we cook the shanks low and slow in crock pot with a smatering of veg and earthy game stock.

Ingredients

  • 2 venison shanks if using larger animals such as elk or mooose or a large whitetail, use 4 shanks if you have a smaller animal in the freezer
  • Game stock
  • carrots
  • celery
  • onion
  • potatoes
  • parsley
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Heat cast iron pan to smoking hot
  2. Coat shanks in oil salt and pepper liberally
  3. Sear shanks on all side and put in crockpot
  4. Chop all veg into large chunks and add to crockpot
  5. Add game stock to pot
  6. Set crockpot to low and let cook for 6-8 hours
  7. Add aromatic spices in the last hour of cooking
